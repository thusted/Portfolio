SONIC STEPS

A SOUND-BASED INSTALLATION WHERE PARTICIPANTS ARE WELCOMED TO WALK ACROSS A GRID OF STEPPING STONES


Comissioned for and featured at Wilsonville Festival of Arts 2018. Sonic Steps is a sound-based installation where participants were welcomed to walk across a grid of stepping stones, each triggering a different sound. As more would people join, they beame a part of a collective, improvisatory composition, combining different sonic textures through movement.

Technologies Used
  • Arduino Uno
  • Arduino IDE
  • MaxMSP
  • Ableton Live
  • Force Sensitive Resistors
