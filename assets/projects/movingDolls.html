THE MOVING DOLLS

A ROBOTICS PIECE INSPIRED BY THE RISE OF THE SPIRITUALISM MOVEMENT AT THE TURN OF THE 19TH CENTURY


“The Moving Dolls” is a robotics piece inspired by the rise of Spiritualism movement at the turn of the 19th century. Featured at Jacobs Gallery for Manual Experience exhibition, 2020. This piece utilized facial recognition software (OpenCV) to track the movement of participants, relaying that information to servo motors sewn into the bodies of thirteen dolls on a shelf.

Technologies Used
  • Arduino Uno
  • Arduino IDE
  • MaxMSP
  • OpenCV
  • Servo Motors
  • Relay Modules
