The Moving Dolls

“The Moving Dolls” is a robotics piece inspired by the rise of Spiritualism movement at the turn of the 19th century. Featured at Jacobs Gallery for Manual Experience exhibition, 2020.

The Dollhouse

Modeled after a turn of the century Victorian home, "The Dollhouse" was a piece comissioned for and featured at Burning Man 2019.

VIEW PROJECT

Sonic Steps

"Sonic Steps" is a sound-based installation where participants were welcomed to walk across a grid of stepping stones, each triggering a different sound. Commissioned for and featured at Wilsonville Festival of Arts 2018.

VIEW PROJECT

Particles

"Particles" is a visual representation of the human voice in the form of animated particles. These particles are visualized through projection mapping onto a an arrangement of spray-painted boxes.

VIEW PROJECT

Binaries

"Binaries" is an interactive painting, using capacitive touch sensors that trigger sound.
